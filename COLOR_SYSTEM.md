## Vibrant Category Colors (POP!)

These colors contrast beautifully with the earthy tones and make the categories stand out:

- **Visual**: #FF6B9D (Hot Pink) - Eye-catching and vibrant
- **Music**: #9D4EDD (Purple) - Rich and bold
- **Story**: #06B6D4 (Cyan) - Fresh and modern
- **Character**: #F59E0B (Amber) - Warm and inviting

## Usage

All colors are available as CSS variables:
- `var(--background)` - Main background
- `var(--foreground)` - Main text
- `var(--category-visual)` - Visual category badge
- `var(--category-music)` - Music category badge
- `var(--category-story)` - Story category badge
- `var(--category-character)` - Character category badge
- `var(--rating-overall)` - Overall rating color
- etc.
